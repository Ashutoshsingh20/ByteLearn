# ByteLearn 📚

**AI-Powered Microlearning for Busy Professionals**

ByteLearn is a modern web application that combines microlearning with AI-driven personalization, enabling professionals to master new skills in just 5-8 minute sessions.

## 🚀 Features

- **⚡ Quick Bite-Sized Lessons**: Learn in focused 5-8 minute chunks that fit your busy schedule
- **🤖 AI-Powered Recommendations**: Intelligent learning paths adapted to your progress and goals
- **🎯 Personalized Experience**: AI tutors track your learning style and recommend optimal content
- **🏆 Gamified Learning**: Earn points, build streaks, and stay motivated with achievement badges
- **📊 Progress Tracking**: Monitor your learning journey with detailed analytics
- **🔥 Streak System**: Build daily learning habits and maintain momentum

## 🎨 Tech Stack

- **React 18** - Modern UI library
- **TypeScript** - Type-safe development
- **Vite** - Lightning-fast build tool
- **CSS3** - Custom styling with modern design

## 🌟 Why ByteLearn?

In 2026, professionals need to upskill constantly but have limited time. ByteLearn solves this by:

1. **Breaking knowledge into digestible chunks** - No more overwhelming hour-long courses
2. **Using AI to optimize your learning** - Learn what matters most for your career
3. **Making learning fun** - Gamification keeps you engaged and motivated
4. **Fitting into your schedule** - Learn during coffee breaks, commutes, or downtime
